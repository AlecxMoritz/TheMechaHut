BattleBots Hiatus
We are very sorry to announce that our BattleBots program will be on an indefinite hiatus for the foreseeable future. We appologize to our pilots, mechanics, and everyone envolved with our BattleBots program, and our loyal fans. The stadium housing the BattleBots tournaments was destroyed during our Spring Invitational, and the damage is extensive. We are happy to report that the stadium was evacuated and no one was seriously hurt, and the pilots were able to leave the stadium in their suits to protect themselves from injury. We have contactors and builders working to get us back up and running, but it is a massive project. We thank you for your patience and cannot wait to see what you'll bring back for us next year!
